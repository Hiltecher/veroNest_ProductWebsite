veroNest Features

Designed to optimize energy usage and enhance care home efficiency.

Appliance Control

Connect, remove, and control appliances with ease, ensuring efficient energy use.

Connect Devices: Easily add appliances to the system for monitoring.

Remove Devices: Safely disconnect appliances when no longer needed.

Change State: Turn appliances on or off remotely.

Schedule Usage: Set automatic schedules to optimize energy use.

Energy Monitoring

Track real-time energy consumption with interactive graphs and reports.

Live Data: View energy consumption in real time.

Graph Reports: Get visual insights into usage trends.

Usage Alerts: Receive notifications if energy consumption is too high.

Cost Savings: Identify opportunities to reduce expenses.

Account Management

Manage user accounts with role-based access for residents and staff.

Create Accounts: Easily onboard new staff and residents.

Role Management: Assign different permissions for security.

Edit Details: Update user information and preferences.

Remove Users: Deactivate accounts when necessary.

Smart Automation

Automate energy-saving routines to reduce unnecessary consumption.

Smart Lighting: Automatically turn off unused lights.

Temperature Control: Adjust room temperatures based on time of day.

Motion Sensors: Detect activity and control devices accordingly.

Automated Scheduling: Set daily routines for energy efficiency.
