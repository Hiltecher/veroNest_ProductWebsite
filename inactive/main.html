VeroNest


Smarter energy monitoring for every care home.

The next step in efficient home care.


VeroNest is an energy monitoring solution designed specifically for care homes. By providing real-time insights and analytics, VeroNest helps care facilities optimize energy consumption, reduce costs, and promote sustainability.

The key features.


  • Real-time energy monitoring.
  • Automated alerts for unusual energy usage.
  • Detailed analytics and reporting.
  • Cost-saving recommendations.
  • Easy integration with existing systems.

How it works.


VeroNest connects to your care home's energy infrastructure, gathering data and providing actionable insights through a user-friendly dashboard. Our system ensures you stay in control of energy consumption without added complexity.

Grow your tree.


VeroNest encourages efficient energy usage by introducing a virtual tree, that grows depending on how much energy is saved.
